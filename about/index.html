It's me, hi.

I’m Cullen, and I’m enchanted to have you here! By day, I’m a software engineer and web developer, but when I’m not coding, I’m a dedicated Swiftie of course.

For a long time, I searched for a way to easily rank my favorite songs and organize tracks by album, but never really found something I liked. So, I decided to create my own solution!

The Swiftie Ranking Hub let's you rank your top 13 songs, categorize tracks by album, and much more. The best part? Everything you do will be saved, so you can return and indulge in your rankings over and over (especially when Taylor drops new music).

One of my favorite things about being a Swiftie is the supportive community that surrounds us. To celebrate this, I’ve added the ability to share your top 13 songs list, custom profile page and dream Eras Tour with friends. I hope this helps us connect and share our love for Taylor even more 🫶

In case you're curious, check out my profile to see my favorite songs and dream Eras Tour. Can't wait to see what all of you come up with too!
