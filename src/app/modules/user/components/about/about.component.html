It's me, hi.

I’m Cullen, and I’m enchanted to have you here! By day, I’m a software engineer and web developer, but when I’m not coding, I’m a dedicated Swiftie.

For a long time, I searched for a way to easily rank my favorite songs and organize tracks by album, but nothing quite fit the bill. So, I decided to create my own solution.

Here, you can rank your top 13 songs, categorize tracks by album, and much more! The best part? Everything you do will be saved, so you can return and indulge in your rankings (especially when Taylor drops new music).

One of my favorite things about being a Swiftie is the supportive community that surrounds us. To celebrate this, I’ve added the option to share your top 13 songs list and dream eras tour with friends, helping us connect and share our love for Taylor even more.

This project is a true labor of love, and I’m excited to share it with you. It’s completely free, but if you’d like to buy me a coffee as a token of appreciation, I’d be incredibly grateful!
